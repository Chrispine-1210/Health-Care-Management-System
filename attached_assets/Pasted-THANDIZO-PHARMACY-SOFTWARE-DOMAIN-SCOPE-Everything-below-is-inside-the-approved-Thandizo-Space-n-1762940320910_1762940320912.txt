THANDIZO PHARMACY SOFTWARE DOMAIN SCOPE
Everything below is inside the approved “Thandizo Space” — nothing outside this ecosystem.

1. Pharmacy Operations Core
Purpose: Handle all prescription, dispensing, and stock operations across all Thandizo outlets.
Modules:
Prescription Management – create, upload (scan), validate, and fulfil prescriptions.
Dispensing Workflow – pharmacist validation, dosage tracking, substitution, expiry checks.
Drug Catalogue & Inventory – complete product database: batch, expiry, supplier, storage condition.
Stock Movement Tracking – between branches, from supplier to store, or returns.
Automated Reordering – reorder triggers when stock hits threshold.
Drug Interaction/Allergy Alerts – real-time warnings to avoid dispensing errors.

2. Retail & Point-of-Sale (POS)
Purpose: Manage walk-in sales, billing, and financial reconciliation.
Modules:
Multi-branch POS with local and online modes (works even if internet drops).
Integrated pricing engine with promotions, discounts, and loyalty points.
Accepts cash, mobile money (Airtel, Mpamba), POS cards, digital wallets.
Generates e-invoices, receipts, and tax-compliant reports (MRA-ready).
Real-time sync with central server for analytics and stock updates.

3. Patient & Customer Portal
Purpose: Empower patients and regular customers to manage their health and orders.
Modules:
Secure registration & health profile (medications, allergies, chronic conditions).
Order prescription drugs or OTC medicines online.
Request consultations (video/audio or in-pharmacy).
Receive refill reminders & medication adherence alerts.
Access medical summaries, invoices, loyalty points, and delivery status.
Social engagement (likes, comments, health blog interactions).

4. Consultation & Clinical Services
Purpose: Enable virtual or in-person medical advice and prescription generation.
Modules:
Video/Chat consultation between doctor/pharmacist and patient.
Prescription auto-generation tied to the pharmacy inventory.
Doctor verification and licensing checks before prescribing.
Patient record & consultation logs (immutable and auditable).
Integration with lab results and referral management.

5. Delivery & Logistics
Purpose: Manage last-mile delivery of medicines and services.
Modules:
Geo-location-based delivery management.
Distance-based cost computation (auto).
Delivery agent app (accept/reject tasks, mark delivered, get payment).
Real-time tracking, proof of delivery (signature/photo).
Delivery performance analytics.

6. Branding, Communication & Marketing Hub
Purpose: Build and control the Thandizo digital identity across all platforms.
Modules:
Central Brand Portal — houses logos, certificates, licenses, brand guidelines, accreditations.
Blog & News System — health articles, public awareness, auto-post to social media.
Social Embed Engine — posts automatically sync from Facebook, Instagram, X (Twitter).
Editor Tools — advanced WYSIWYG editor for text, color, font, layout, media embeds.
SEO optimization for all content.
Campaign management (email/SMS/WhatsApp push).

7. Regulatory & Compliance
Purpose: Maintain trust, traceability, and legal protection.
Modules:
Store and track all pharmacy, medical, and corporate licenses.
Expiry alerts for licenses, pharmacist registration, and insurance.
Digital audit trail for every transaction, prescription, or consultation.
Role-based data access (strict segregation: admin vs pharmacist vs customer).
Integration hooks for PMRA, MRA, and future e-health regulations.
Data privacy controls (GDPR & Malawi equivalent compliance).

8. Administration & Enterprise Control
Purpose: Empower head office to control all multi-branch operations centrally.
Modules:
Super-admin dashboard — complete system visibility.
Branch setup, staff assignment, access control, permissions.
User management (roles, groups, departments, pharmacies, riders).
File manager for photos, videos, docs, reports, certificates, product images.
Data-driven performance dashboard — sales, stock turnover, delivery efficiency.
Content publishing approval workflows.
Security monitoring and backup management.

9. Analytics & Business Intelligence
Purpose: Turn operations into strategic insights.
Modules:
Predictive analytics: forecast demand, optimize purchasing, avoid stockouts.
Drug consumption trend analysis (by region, age, season).
Profitability tracking per product/branch.
Customer retention, loyalty tracking, and churn prediction.
Power BI or in-app dashboards with exportable visual reports.

10. Technology Infrastructure
Purpose: Ensure scalability, performance, and high availability.
Architecture:
Microservices-based (no monorepo).
Frontend: React or Vue. Backend: Node.js + Express or NestJS.
Database: PostgreSQL + Redis cache.
Cloud: AWS/Azure hybrid with CDN + regional data redundancy.
Message queue (RabbitMQ/Kafka) for orders and notifications.
Storage: S3-compatible for media, PDFs, and documents.
CI/CD for continuous deployment and updates.
API gateway for third-party integrations.
Ai integration
